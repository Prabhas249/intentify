## Content Guidelines

### Key Messages (don't repeat across sections)
1. **Hero** - Value prop: "Turn high-intent visitors into paying customers"
2. **Workflow header** - Intelligence: "Understand your visitors before you engage"
3. **Workflow sidebar** - Ease: "Go live in 2 minutes"
4. **Workflow blocks** - How it works: Setup + Real-time tracking
5. **Workflow connect** - Targeting: "Right visitor. Right moment."

### Differentiation Points
- Visitor memory (remembers returning visitors)
- Intent scoring (low/medium/high)
- Source attribution (UTM, referrer)
- WhatsApp popups (India-first)
- INR pricing (affordable)
- Lightweight script (~5KB)

### Tone
- Direct, confident
- No fluff
- India D2C focused
- Technical but accessible
